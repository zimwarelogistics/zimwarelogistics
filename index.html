About Us

  • We are ZimWareLogistics, a leading logistics, warehouse and supply company in Zimbabwe. We help other companies get the products they need from the best suppliers, store them in our secure and spacious warehouses, and deliver them to their locations across the nation and beyond. We started our business in 2008 with a vision to provide efficient and affordable logistics services to the retail sector in Zimbabwe. Since then, we have grown to become one of the most trusted and reputable companies in the industry, serving hundreds of companies and suppliers. We have a team of experienced and dedicated professionals who handle every aspect of our operations, from sourcing, warehousing, transportation, to customer service. We use the latest technology and equipment to ensure the quality and safety of the products we handle. We also adhere to the highest standards of ethics and sustainability in our business practices. Our mission is to help our clients grow their businesses by providing them with reliable and cost-effective logistics solutions. We aim to build long-term and mutually beneficial relationships with our clients, suppliers, and partners. We are committed to excellence, innovation, and customer satisfaction. If you are looking for a logistics partner that can handle your storage and supply needs, look no further than ZimWareLogistics. Contact us today and let us show you how we can help you.
